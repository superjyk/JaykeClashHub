Our Story

From player to trusted seller.

Jayke Clash Store started from a simple frustration. As a longtime Clash of Clans player, Jayke knew how hard it was to find a trustworthy place to buy or sell accounts — most platforms were filled with scammers, inflated prices, and zero accountability.

So instead of complaining about it, he built a solution. What started as selling accounts to friends quickly grew into a full store, powered by word-of-mouth and one simple promise: every account is real, every deal is fair, every delivery is fast.

Today, Jayke Clash Store serves thousands of players across the Philippines and beyond — from beginners looking for a head start to veterans chasing that max TH18 endgame.
