Important Rules
Please read before choosing an installment plan.

🔒 Account Held Until Full Payment

For 3-Part Plans, the account is held in reserve until payment is completed in full. Partial access may be discussed case by case.

📋 Deposit Required

All installment plans require a deposit to officially reserve your chosen account. No deposit = no reservation.

⏱️ Payment Deadlines

Late payments may result in loss of reservation. Please message us ahead of time if you need more flexibility.

🤝 Flexible on a Case-by-Case Basis

We're people too. If you're having issues, just talk to us. We'll find something that works for both sides.

⚠️ Installment plans are only available via direct message on Facebook or WhatsApp. All plans must be agreed upon in writing before account reservation begins.
